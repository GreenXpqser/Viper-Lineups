Viper is a predator that deals in poison and chemical warfare. She has exploding projectiles that spill acid on the battlefield, as well as a poison-gas emitter, and a toxic gas screen to prevent passage. Her ultimate emits a toxic cloud that conceals her location and can highlight enemies who wander into it. Viper ‎controls sites very well by smoking and blocking out different areas of the map using her abilities. Viper ‎also requires a high skill ceiling to play her with full potential. Learning and remembering lineups and positioning is required to play Viper the way she is intended to play. This website is a compilation of different lineups including one way smokes, toxic screens, plant setup, map lockdown, pre/post-plant smokes and snakebites, viper's pits and more.
